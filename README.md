I developed a Bevyxxa/betrixaban patient-identification and utilization framework that used administrative claims and EHR prescribing data to capture real-world treatment activity for Bayer across medical, pharmacy, and clinical-record sources. The goal was to identify members with Bevyxxa or betrixaban exposure, pull all relevant facility, physician, pharmacy, and EHR prescription records over the analysis window, and assemble a unified view of product use, provider involvement, site of care, diagnosis context, and dispensing or administration detail so the brand’s early market activity could be profiled across both claims and EHR environments.
